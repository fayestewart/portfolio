About Faye

Faye Stewart

Graphic Designer & Adventurer

Faye is currently studying for an Honours Degree at the University of Sunderland, having already gained a Foundation Degree in Graphic Design in her hometown of Newcastle-Upon-Tyne.

As well as being an keen photographer and explorer, She is a quick learner and is always looking to discover new techniques and design processes. The areas of graphic design she has a special interested in are branding and editorial design.

Brand Identity

Creating unique brand identities is her passion and it's something She's good at.

Ideas & Development

She is always looking for new inspiration and can find design ideas in even the most unlikely of places.

Social
